NUS Physics Illustratum

2022

NUS Physics Illustratum is a competition organised by NUS Physics Department in which participants are tasked to present to a panel on any topic in the area of Physics.

2022-2023

In this project, my team did research on the feasibility of installing piezoelectric tiles that are typically used on the pavement on car roads and motorways as an alternative way to generate energy.
